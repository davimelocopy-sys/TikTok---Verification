## Dimension Scores & Analysis

### 1. Operational Efficiency: 54/100
**Category**: Significant Efficiency Gaps

#### Key Findings
- **Time Entry**: Attorneys record time weekly (not daily), leading to 22% unbilled time
- **Document Search**: Average 15 minutes to locate relevant precedents
- **System Fragmentation**: 4 separate systems with manual data transfer
- **Template Access**: Templates stored on individual attorney drives, no central library

#### Specific Impacts
- **Weekly time entry** results in forgotten billable activities worth ~$3,800/week
- **Precedent search time** costs 12 attorney hours/week ($6,000/week in opportunity cost)
- **Manual data re-entry** creates 8 hours/week of administrative burden

#### Recommendations (Priority Order)
1. **Implement daily time entry** (Impact: High | Effort: Low)
   - Target: Reduce unbilled time from 22% to <10%
   - Estimated recovery: $150K-$180K annually
   - Implementation: Mobile time entry app, daily reminders

2. **Centralize document templates** (Impact: Medium | Effort: Low)
   - Target: Reduce search time from 15 min to <3 min
   - Estimated savings: 10 hours/week
   - Implementation: Cloud-based template library with tagging

3. **Integrate core systems** (Impact: High | Effort: High)
   - Target: Eliminate 3 of 4 manual data transfers
   - Estimated savings: 6 hours/week
   - Implementation: Practice management system with CRM integration

---

### 2. Revenue Protection: 48/100
**Category**: Significant Revenue Leakage

#### Key Findings
- **Unbilled Time**: 22% of worked time never invoiced ($198K annual loss)
- **Billing Cycle**: 8 days from month-end to invoice delivery
- **Write-downs**: 12% average write-down rate
- **AR Aging**: 28% of invoices outstanding >60 days
- **Payment Options**: Check and wire only (no credit card/ACH)

#### Specific Impacts
- **22% unbilled time** = $198K in unrecovered revenue annually
- **8-day billing cycle** delays cash flow by $45K-$60K monthly
- **Limited payment options** slow collections by 15-20 days on average

#### Recommendations (Priority Order)
1. **Reduce unbilled time** (Impact: Critical | Effort: Low)
   - Target: <10% unbilled time
   - Estimated recovery: $150K-$180K annually
   - Implementation: Daily time entry + weekly review process

2. **Accelerate billing cycle** (Impact: High | Effort: Medium)
   - Target: 3-day billing cycle
   - Estimated impact: $45K-$60K faster monthly cash flow
   - Implementation: Automated time approval workflow, pre-bill review

3. **Add payment options** (Impact: Medium | Effort: Low)
   - Target: 50% of clients using automated payment
   - Estimated impact: 15-20 day faster collections
   - Implementation: Credit card processing, ACH setup
